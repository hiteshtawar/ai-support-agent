# Account and Login

## Logging In

Go to **sampleapp.com/login** and enter your email and password. If your
organisation uses SSO (Single Sign-On), click **Sign in with SSO** and enter
your company domain.

## Forgot Password or Locked Out

If you cannot log in:

1. Go to **sampleapp.com/login**.
2. Click **Forgot password?** below the login form.
3. Enter your email address and click **Send Reset Link**.
4. Check your inbox for an email from `noreply@sampleapp.com`. If it does not
   arrive within 2 minutes, check your spam or junk folder.
5. Click the link in the email and set a new password. The link expires after
   30 minutes.

If you do not receive the reset email and it is not in spam, your account may be
registered under a different email address. Try other email addresses you may
have used to sign up.

## Resetting Your Password While Logged In

1. Go to **Settings → Profile → Security**.
2. Click **Change Password**.
3. Enter your current password, then your new password twice.
4. Click **Save**.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

To enable 2FA:

1. Go to **Settings → Profile → Security → Two-Factor Authentication**.
2. Click **Enable 2FA**.
3. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.).
4. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm setup.

If you lose access to your 2FA device, use one of the recovery codes provided
during setup. If you do not have recovery codes, create a support ticket with
proof of identity.

## Updating Your Profile

Go to **Settings → Profile** to update your name, email address, job title,
and profile photo.

**Changing your email address:** Enter the new address and click Save. A
confirmation email will be sent to the new address. The change takes effect
after you click the confirmation link.

## Deleting Your Account

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible.

1. Go to **Settings → Profile → Danger Zone**.
2. Click **Delete Account**.
3. Type your email address to confirm.
4. Click **Permanently Delete**.

All your data, reports, integrations, and team members will be removed immediately.
We recommend exporting your data before deleting. Once deleted, accounts cannot
be restored even within the 30-day window that applies to cancellations.

## Session Timeout

Sessions expire after 30 days of inactivity by default. Admins can adjust the
session timeout for the whole workspace under **Settings → Security → Session
Policy**.
