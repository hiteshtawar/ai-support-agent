# Team Management

## Inviting a Team Member

1. Go to **Settings → Team**.
2. Click **Invite Member**.
3. Enter the person's email address.
4. Choose their role:
   - **Admin** — full access including billing, settings, and user management.
   - **Editor** — can create, edit, and delete reports and data. Cannot manage users or billing.
   - **Viewer** — read-only access to reports and dashboards. Cannot create or edit anything.
5. Click **Send Invite**.

The invitee receives an email with a link to accept the invitation and set up
their account. Invitations expire after 7 days. You can resend an expired
invitation from **Settings → Team → Pending Invitations**.

## Changing a Team Member's Role

1. Go to **Settings → Team**.
2. Find the team member and click the role dropdown next to their name.
3. Select the new role.
4. The change takes effect immediately.

You cannot change the role of the workspace owner without transferring ownership.

## Removing a Team Member

1. Go to **Settings → Team**.
2. Find the team member and click the three-dot menu next to their name.
3. Click **Remove from Workspace**.
4. Confirm the removal.

The removed member loses access immediately. Their reports and data remain in
the workspace and are reassigned to the admin who performed the removal.

## Transferring Workspace Ownership

The workspace owner is the account that created the workspace and holds the
billing relationship.

1. Go to **Settings → Team**.
2. Find the member you want to transfer ownership to (they must be an Admin).
3. Click the three-dot menu → **Transfer Ownership**.
4. Enter your password to confirm.

After transfer, your role becomes Admin. Only the new owner can transfer
ownership again.

## Team Member Can't Accept the Invitation

Common causes:

- **Invitation expired** — resend from Settings → Team → Pending Invitations.
- **Wrong email** — the invitation link only works for the specific email it was
  sent to. If the person uses a different email address, remove the invitation
  and resend to the correct address.
- **Email in spam** — ask them to check their spam folder for an email from
  `noreply@sampleapp.com`.
- **Already a member** — if the person already has a Sample App account under
  that email, they should log in first and then accept the invitation.
