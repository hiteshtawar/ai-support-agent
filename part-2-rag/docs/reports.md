# Reports

## Creating a Report

1. Click **Reports** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **New Report** in the top-right corner.
3. Choose a report type: Summary, Detailed, Trend, or Custom.
4. Configure the date range, filters, and grouping options.
5. Click **Run Report**.

Reports run in real time. Large reports covering long date ranges may take up to
30 seconds to generate.

## Exporting a Report

You can export any report to CSV, PDF, or Excel.

1. Open the report you want to export.
2. Click the **Export** button in the top-right corner of the report view.
3. Select your format:
   - **CSV** — raw data, best for further analysis in Excel or Google Sheets.
   - **PDF** — formatted for printing or sharing with stakeholders.
   - **Excel (.xlsx)** — includes formatting and multiple sheets for complex reports.
4. The file downloads automatically to your default downloads folder.

For very large reports, the export runs as a background job. You will receive an
email with a download link when it is ready (usually within 5 minutes).

## Scheduling a Report

Reports can be emailed on a schedule:

1. Open the report.
2. Click **Schedule** in the top-right corner.
3. Set the frequency (daily, weekly, monthly) and the delivery time.
4. Enter the email addresses to send the report to.
5. Click **Save Schedule**.

Scheduled reports are sent in PDF format. To change the format or edit
recipients, return to the Schedule settings on the report.

## Sharing a Report

To share a report with a teammate:

1. Open the report.
2. Click **Share** in the top-right corner.
3. Choose **Share with team** (visible to all workspace members) or enter
   specific email addresses.
4. Click **Copy link** to get a direct URL you can paste anywhere.

Shared links require the recipient to be logged into Sample App. For external
sharing, export to PDF and share the file directly.

## Report Shows No Data

If a report returns no results:

- Check the date range — the default is "Last 30 days". Widen it if needed.
- Check the filters applied. A misconfigured filter can exclude all results.
- Verify that data exists in the system for the time period selected.
- If you recently started using Sample App, there may not be enough data yet.

## Deleting a Report

1. Go to **Reports**.
2. Hover over the report and click the three-dot menu.
3. Click **Delete**.

Deletion is permanent. Scheduled reports associated with the deleted report will
also be cancelled.
