# Billing

## Plans

Sample App offers three plans:

- **Starter** — up to 3 users, core reporting features, email support.
- **Pro** — unlimited users, advanced analytics, priority support, API access.
- **Enterprise** — custom limits, SSO, dedicated account manager, SLA.

All plans are billed monthly or annually. Annual billing saves 20%.

## How to Upgrade Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Change Plan**.
3. Select the plan you want to upgrade to.
4. Confirm your payment details and click **Upgrade Now**.

The upgrade takes effect immediately. You are charged a prorated amount for the
remainder of the current billing period, then the full new plan price from the
next billing date.

## How to Cancel Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Manage Subscription**.
3. Click **Cancel Plan** and follow the confirmation prompts.

Your access continues until the end of the current billing period — you will not
be charged again after cancellation. Your data is retained for 30 days after
cancellation in case you decide to reactivate.

If you are cancelling because of a specific issue, our team is happy to help
before you go. Create a support ticket or email support@sampleapp.com.

## Invoices and Receipts

All invoices are available under **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**.

- Invoices are generated automatically within 24 hours of each payment.
- Each invoice is a PDF you can download for your records.
- If your company needs invoices with a specific billing address or VAT number,
  update your billing details under **Settings → Billing → Billing Information**
  before the next payment date.

## Invoice Looks Wrong or Is Missing

If an invoice shows an incorrect amount:

1. Check that your billing details (plan, seat count, billing address) are correct
   under **Settings → Billing**.
2. If you were recently charged after cancelling, note that cancellation takes
   effect at the end of the billing period, not immediately.
3. If you still believe the charge is wrong, create a support ticket and include
   the invoice number and the billing period in question.

For missing invoices, allow up to 24 hours after payment for them to appear.
Check your spam folder for the emailed receipt.

## Changing Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Method**.
2. Click **Update Card**.
3. Enter your new card details and click **Save**.

The new card will be charged on your next billing date.

## Reactivating a Cancelled Account

If your account was cancelled and you want to reactivate within the 30-day
retention window:

1. Log in with your existing credentials.
2. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
3. Click **Reactivate Plan** and choose a plan.

All your data will be restored exactly as it was at cancellation.
