# Mobile and the Sample App web application

## Is there a native mobile app?

**No.** Sample App is a **web application** you use in a browser — on a laptop,
tablet, or phone. There is **no separate iOS or Android app** to install from
the App Store or Google Play.

When we say “the app” or “Sample App” in documentation, we always mean the
product you open at **sampleapp.com** in **Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge**.

## “The mobile app crashes” or “the app on my phone keeps crashing”

If you are using Sample App **in your phone’s browser** and the page crashes,
closes, or freezes:

1. **Update your mobile browser** to the latest version from the App Store /
   Play Store (Chrome, Safari, etc.).
2. **Force-close the browser tab** and open a fresh tab to sampleapp.com.
3. **Clear the browser’s cache** for sampleapp.com (in the browser settings).
4. **Try from WiFi vs mobile data** — some corporate or carrier networks block
   required domains.
5. **Check status.sampleapp.com** for any active incidents.

These are the same browser troubleshooting steps as on desktop. There is no
separate “mobile app” crash log or build version from Sample Company — support
will ask which **browser** and **OS version** you use.

## If you were looking for a downloadable mobile app

We do not offer one today. Bookmark sampleapp.com on your home screen for quick
access (use “Add to Home Screen” in Safari/Chrome).

For feature requests or enterprise mobile roadmap questions, create a support
ticket.
